# Organizational Breakdown Structure (OBS)

The OBS is a hierarchical representation of your company used throughout Clarity.

## Key Uses
- **Categorize** objects for filtering and reporting
- **Grant access rights** based on OBS membership
- **Align investments** with organizational units
- **Financial** entity association

## Where OBS is Used
- Investment filtering in all workspaces
- Access rights assignment
- Resource organization
- Financial entity structure
- Reporting and analytics
