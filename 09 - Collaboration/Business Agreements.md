# Creating and Managing Business Agreements

Manage formal commitments between investments when they are interdependent.

## Creating an Agreement
1. Navigate to the Agreements module
2. Click + New Agreement
3. Select the Provider investment (delivering work)
4. Select the Consumer investment (receiving deliverable)
5. Define terms, dates, and deliverables

## Agreement Lifecycle
- Draft then Active then Complete (or Cancelled)
