# Track and Edit To Dos

**To Dos** are personal task items for tracking your own work. Unlike investment **Tasks** or **Checklists**, To Dos are scoped to an individual user and are not visible to other team members.

> [!NOTE]
> To Dos can be added to **Checklists** and **Smartlists** within My Workspace. A **Note** icon appears on a To Do card when a note has been entered.

## To Dos vs. Tasks vs. Checklists

| Feature | **To Dos** | **Tasks** | **Checklists** |
|---------|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Scope | Personal | Investment | Investment |
| Visibility | Individual only | Team | Team |
| Effort tracking | No | Yes | No |
| Dependencies | No | Yes | No |

## Working with To Dos

- To Dos can be organized in **Checklists** and **Smartlists** in My Workspace.
- You can view To Dos in **list**, **board**, and **calendar** layouts.
- You can enter a maximum of **1333 characters** in the **Note** field.
- You can view a maximum of **500 cards** in all Smartlist layouts.

> [!WARNING]
> The **calendar layout** does not support custom keyboard navigation (508 compliance) in this release. The **Attachment** option is also not available in the calendar layout.

## Attachments on To Dos

An out-of-the-box **Attachments** attribute is available for To Dos in Checklists, Smartlists, and the To Dos workspace. The attribute is system-defined and its configuration is not customizable.

- In **Checklists** and **Smartlists**: the three-dot action menu includes an **Attachments** option for uploading files.
- In the **To Dos workspace**: the **Attachment** field is available in the **Column** panel and can be configured to upload files.
