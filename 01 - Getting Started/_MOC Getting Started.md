# Getting Started with Clarity

> [!important]
> In this documentation, **Modern UX** is referred to as **Clarity**, and the **Classic UI** is referred to as **Classic PPM**.

## Overview

Clarity is Broadcom's digital product and portfolio management platform. It transforms organizations from managing one-off projects to developing initiatives as a true product or program portfolio.

**Three pillars of Clarity:**
- **Simple** — Engage business leaders with drag-and-drop roadmaps. Add teams, costs, risks, and dependencies with a few clicks.
- **Usable** — PMO collaborates with resource managers to find the right team based on skill, geography, and availability. Team members update timesheets on mobile.
- **Powerful** — Excel-like financial management with planned, actual, and forecasted budgets in real-time.

## Key Concepts in Clarity

Clarity organizes investments the way your business operates. Instead of running one-off projects, you see money, people, and work by product or service lines.

### Investment Types
| Type | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **[[_MOC Projects\|Projects]]** | Large initiatives with complex tasks managed daily by teams |
| **Ideas** | Creative ideas captured and prototyped before converting to projects |
| **[[_MOC Investments\|Custom Investments]]** | Flexible investment types aligned with your terminology |
| **Teams** | Persistent, long-term funded teams organized around products and value streams |

### Core Modules
- **[[_MOC Financials\|Financials]]** — Budget tracking with planned, actual, and forecasted costs
- **[[Tasks Phases Milestones\|Tasks]]** — Task assignment, tracking, and completion management
- **[[Status Reports Overview\|Status]]** — Create, manage, and publish status reports
- **Risks, Issues, Changes** — Track risks, issues, and change requests
- **Checklists** — Track required steps throughout the lifecycle
- **[[Business Agreements\|Agreements]]** — Manage interdependencies between investments
- **[[_MOC Resources\|Staffing]]** — Resource allocation across all investment types
- **[[_MOC Timesheets\|Timesheets]]** — Track actual time spent on investments
- **[[_MOC Reporting\|Reporting]]** — Build and publish reports and dashboards
