# Working with Plans (Beta)

## Overview
The Plans workspace enables you to analyze historical workforce data, identify employee turnover or skills demand patterns, and predict future workforce needs.

## Plan Types
- OBS-Driven Plans — Analyze workforce by organizational structure
- Resource-Driven Actuals Plans — Track actual resource utilization

## Creating an OBS-Driven Plan
1. Navigate to the Plans workspace
2. Create a new plan
3. Select the OBS unit to analyze
4. Configure time periods and metrics
5. Synchronize with investment data
