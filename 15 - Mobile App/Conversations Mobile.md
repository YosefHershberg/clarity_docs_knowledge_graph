# Conversations (Mobile)

## Overview

Use Conversations in the Clarity Mobile app to view discussion threads, post replies, upload attachments, and edit/delete your own content.

## Prerequisites

You need the `My Workspace - Navigate` access right.

## View Conversations

1. Log in to the Clarity Mobile app.
2. Tap `Menu` > `Conversations`.

> [!note]
> The list includes threads where you:
> - started a conversation,
> - replied to a thread, or
> - were mentioned by another user.
>
> Each conversation card shows context/object, last updated date, and user avatar.

## Post a Comment

1. Tap `Menu` > `Conversations`.
2. Open the target conversation card.
3. Enter text in the comment box.
4. Tap `Post`.

## Upload an Attachment to a Comment

1. Tap `Menu` > `Conversations`.
2. Open a conversation card.
3. Tap `Attach` below the comment box.
4. Choose one option:
   - Upload a file from the device.
   - Open the camera to capture photo/video.
5. Tap `Post`.

> [!note]
> Only one attachment can be added at a time, with a maximum of five attachments per comment.

To delete a selected attachment before posting, tap the `Trash` icon.

## Edit or Delete a Comment or Entire Conversation

The thread/comment author can edit or delete using left-swipe gestures.

### Edit/Delete a comment

1. Tap `Menu` > `Conversations`.
2. Open a conversation card.
3. Swipe left on the comment.
4. Choose `Edit` or `Delete`.

### Edit/Delete an entire conversation thread

1. Tap `Menu` > `Conversations`.
2. Open a conversation card.
3. Swipe left on the thread.
4. Choose `Edit` or `Delete`.
