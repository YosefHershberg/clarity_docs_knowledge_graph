# Getting Started with Mobile App

## Overview

This page covers:

- Requirements
- Required access rights and settings
- Download and configuration
- What to do next

## Requirements

- iOS 16.0 or higher (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch)
- Android 12.0 or higher
- Wireless or data network access
- Supported Clarity SaaS or On-Premise release

### Release compatibility

| Your Clarity release | Mobile app support |
|---|---|
| 16.0.3 or higher | Yes |
| 16.0.2 or earlier | No |

> [!important]
> To manage Frictionless (virtual) time entries from Rally in Mobile App 3.3.1, use Clarity 16.3.1 or higher.
>
> To use Conversations in Mobile App 3.3.1, use Clarity 16.2.3 or higher.

## Required Access Rights and Settings

Contact your administrator to confirm required rights and settings in Clarity so you can:

- submit your own timesheets,
- approve timesheets (if applicable), and
- access the relevant mobile menus.

Administrators can also refer to Clarity access-rights documentation in the Reference section.

## Download and Configure the App

Before downloading, verify that you are a licensed Clarity SaaS or on-premise user with required access rights.

1. Open the Apple App Store or Google Play Store on your device.
2. Download `Clarity Mobile`.
3. Open the app.
4. Configure a connection to your Clarity environment.

## Frictionless Time Entry Highlights

- View Rally-based virtual time entries in the selected timesheet period.
- Edit actual hours, add notes, or delete entries.
- Submit timesheets with Frictionless entries the same way as standard entries.

> [!note]
> Virtual entries are automatically converted to tasks and assignments after submit/post.

### Add Task from Assignment (with ETC)

- Use `Add Task from assignments (with ETC)` from the timesheet action menu.
- Tasks with ETC are added and prefilled with actual hours.
- If blank entries exist, the app prompts before replacement.

> [!note]
> Ensure `Populate assigned tasks (with ETC)` is enabled in Classic PPM Timesheet Options.
