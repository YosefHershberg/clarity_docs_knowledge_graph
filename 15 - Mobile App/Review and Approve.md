# Review and Approve (Mobile)

## Overview

As a manager, you can review submitted timesheets, approve them, or return them for correction.

> [!note]
> You need Clarity 15.9.2 or higher and Clarity Mobile App 3.1.2 or higher to use Review and Approve in mobile.

After login, tap `Menu` to open `Review & Approve`.

The page defaults to the current period. Swipe left/right to navigate periods.

## Period Statuses

Each period includes these statuses:

- Open: Not yet submitted.
- Returned: Returned to user and must be updated and resubmitted.
- Submitted: Awaiting approval/return action.
- Approved: Already approved.

## Work with Timesheets by Status

### 1. Open timesheets

1. Tap `Open` to expand.
2. Select a timesheet.
3. Use `Total` tab for full period view.
4. Swipe left/right to move between days.
5. Tap a day to filter tasks for that day.

Available actions:

- `Remind` to notify the user (multiple reminders are allowed).
- Navigation arrows for previous/next timesheet.
- Back icon to return to Review & Approve.

> [!important]
> Timesheet error indicator behavior:
> - Timesheets that cannot be submitted due to timesheet rules show an error icon.
> - The icon is visible in Review & Approve within individual timesheets.
> - The icon remains until the user fixes errors and resubmits.

### 2. Returned timesheets

1. Tap `Returned` to expand.
2. Select a timesheet to review.

### 3. Submitted timesheets

1. Tap `Submitted` to expand.
2. Select a timesheet.
3. Tap a task name to view task details and user notes.
4. Reply to notes as needed.
5. Swipe left to edit/delete your own notes.

After review:

- Tap `Approve` to approve.
- Tap `Return` to send back for correction.
- If approved by mistake, tap `Return` to recall.

### 4. Approved timesheets

1. Tap `Approved` to expand.
2. Select a timesheet.
3. Review details.
4. Tap `Return` if correction is required.

## Main Menu Information

From the main menu, you can:

- View current Clarity server URL
- View current mobile app version
- Navigate to My Timesheet landing page
- Navigate to Review & Approve landing page
- Log out

## Manage Timesheet Notes in Review and Approve

You can add/reply to timesheet-level notes and edit/delete your own notes.

1. Log in and tap `Menu`.
2. Tap `Review & Approve`.
3. Tap the `Notes` icon.
4. Enter text in `Enter note here` and tap `Post`.
5. Swipe left on your own note to edit/delete.
6. Confirm deletion if prompted.

> [!note]
> The notes counter indicates whether there are notes to review.

## Components on the Review & Approve Page

### Search timesheets

1. Tap `Review & Approve`.
2. Navigate to the required period and tap `Search`.
3. Enter first name, last name, full name, or user ID.

The result expands the status tab containing the matching timesheet.

### Filter timesheets

1. Tap `Review & Approve`.
2. Navigate to the required period and tap `Filter`.
3. Select one or more resource managers.
4. Tap Back arrow.
5. Use `Clear Filter` to remove applied criteria.

> [!important]
> Applied filter criteria persist even after app relaunch or logout/login.

### Bulk actions

1. Tap `Review & Approve`.
2. Navigate to period and open `Submitted` or `Approved`.
3. Tap `Approve All` or `Return All in Status`.
4. Confirm with `OK`.

A confirmation appears and counts refresh by status.
