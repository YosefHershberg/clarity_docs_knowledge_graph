## Key Concepts

### Plan Lifecycle
1. **Cost Plan** — Estimate costs
2. **Promote** — Cost plan becomes Budget Plan
3. **Track** — Actuals recorded via timesheets
4. **Forecast** — Combine actuals + remaining estimates
5. **Benefit Plan** — Track expected returns for ROI
