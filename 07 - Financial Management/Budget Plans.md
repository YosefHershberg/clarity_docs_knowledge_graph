# Working with Budget Plans

## Overview
Budget plans represent the approved spending baseline for an investment.

## Creating a Budget Plan
- Promote from Cost Plan: Select a cost plan then Actions then Promote to Budget
- Manual Creation: Create directly and enter budget amounts

## Budget Tracking
- Compare budget vs. actuals
- Track budget variance (Budget minus Actuals)
- Monitor remaining budget per period
