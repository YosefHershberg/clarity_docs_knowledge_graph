# Working with Forecasts

## Overview
Forecasts combine actual costs (past) with remaining estimates (future) to project total investment cost.

## Key Metrics
- EAC (Estimate at Completion) = Actuals + ETC
- ETC (Estimate to Complete) = Remaining estimated cost
- Variance = Budget minus Forecast
