# Working with Benefit Plans and Actual Transactions

## Overview
Benefit plans track expected returns or revenue from investments, enabling ROI analysis.

## Creating a Benefit Plan
1. Navigate to Financials then Benefit Plans
2. Click + New
3. Enter expected benefits by period
4. Track actual benefits as they materialize
