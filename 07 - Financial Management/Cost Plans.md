# Working with Cost Plans

## Overview
Cost plans estimate the total cost of an investment including labor and non-labor expenses.

## Creating a Cost Plan
1. Navigate to the investment Financials module
2. Click Cost Plans tab
3. Click + New Plan
4. Add cost plan detail lines (by resource, role, or expense type)
5. Enter planned amounts per period

## Populate from Allocations
Auto-fill costs based on staff allocations and rate matrices. Costs calculate from allocation hours multiplied by the resource or role rate.
