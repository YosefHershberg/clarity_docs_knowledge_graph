# Custom Investment Financial Plans

## Overview

The Financials module provides four types of financial plans for managing investment economics.

## Plan Types

| Plan | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Cost Plan** | Plan and track the cost of an investment (labor + non-labor) |
| **Budget Plan** | Approved spending plan, often promoted from a cost plan |
| **Benefit Plan** | Expected returns or revenue from the investment |
| **Forecast** | Updated projection combining actuals with remaining estimates |

## Working with Cost Plans

1. Navigate to the investment's **Financials** module
2. Click **Cost Plans** tab
3. Click **+ New** to create a cost plan
4. Add cost plan lines (by resource, role, or expense type)
5. Enter planned amounts per period

### Populate from Allocations
- Click **Populate** > **From Allocations** to auto-fill costs based on staff allocations and rate matrices

> [!tip]
> Use **Populate from Allocations** to keep cost plans synchronized with staffing decisions.

## Working with Budget Plans

- Budget plans represent the approved spending baseline
- **Promote** a cost plan to budget: Click **Actions** > **Promote to Budget**
- Compare cost vs. budget to track variance

## Working with Forecasts

- Forecasts combine actuals (past) with ETC (future estimates)
- Updated regularly to reflect current project reality
- Variance = Budget - Forecast

## Working with Benefit Plans

- Track expected returns or revenue
- Compare benefit plan vs. cost plan for ROI analysis
