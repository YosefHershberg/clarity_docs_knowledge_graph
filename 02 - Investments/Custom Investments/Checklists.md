# Investment Checklists

## Overview

Checklists help track required steps and completion criteria for investments.

## Creating a Checklist

1. Open the investment's **Checklists** module
2. Click **+ New Checklist**
3. Enter a name and description
4. Add checklist items with descriptions and owners

## Tracking Progress

- Check off items as they are completed
- Completion percentage auto-calculates
- Filter by status: All, Completed, Pending

## Checklist vs To-Do vs Risk

| Feature | Checklist | To-Do | Risk |
|---------|-----------|-------|------|
| Scope | Investment-level | Personal | Investment-level |
| Tracking | % complete | Done/Not done | Score matrix |
| Visibility | Team | Individual | Team |
