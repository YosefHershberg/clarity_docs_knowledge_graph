# Manage Risks, Issues, and Changes for Custom Investments

## Risks
- Potential events that could impact the investment
- Scored by **probability** and **impact** (risk score = probability x impact)
- Can be escalated to **Issues** when they materialize
- Track mitigation strategies and owners

## Issues
- Confirmed problems requiring resolution
- Track priority, owner, resolution target date
- Lifecycle: Open → In Progress → Resolved → Closed

## Change Requests
- Formal requests to modify scope, timeline, or budget
- Workflow: Draft → Submitted → Approved/Rejected
- Post-approval: update tasks, financials, and timeline accordingly

## Managing RIC

1. Open the investment and navigate to the **Risks**, **Issues**, or **Changes** module
2. Click **+** to create a new item
3. Fill in required fields (name, description, owner, status)
4. Set priority/impact/probability as applicable
5. Track and update through the lifecycle

> [!tip]
> Use [[Grids|grid]] filtering and [[Saved Views|saved views]] to focus on high-priority items.
