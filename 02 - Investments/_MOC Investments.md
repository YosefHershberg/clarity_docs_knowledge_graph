# Investments Overview

Investments in Clarity represent the various types of work and initiatives that organizations manage. Clarity provides four key investment types, each designed for different organizational needs.

## Investment Types

| Type | Purpose | Folder |
|------|---------|--------|
| **Custom Investments** | Flexible investment types aligned with your business terminology | [[Getting Started\|Custom Investments]] |
| **Ideas** | Capture, develop, and approve new business initiatives | [[Getting Started\|Ideas]] |
| **Team Investments** | Persistent teams organized around products and value streams | [[Getting Started\|Team Investments]] |
| **[[_MOC Projects\|Projects]]** | Complex initiatives with task management (separate section) | `03 - Projects/` |

## Key Concepts

> [!note] OBS Association
> You can associate investment items with the **Organizational Breakdown Structure (OBS)** to link departments and locations. OBS enables filtering, reporting, and access rights based on organizational hierarchy.

> [!tip] Blueprint Configuration
> Each investment type has a blueprint that controls its layout and available modules. Administrators configure blueprints in [[_MOC Administration|Administration]] > [[Common Capabilities|Blueprints]].
