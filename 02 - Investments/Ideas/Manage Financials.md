# Manage Idea Financials

## Overview

The **Financials** module for Ideas provides the same financial planning capabilities as other investment types, enabling organizations to evaluate the financial viability of ideas before committing to full projects.
Financial plans for ideas are used during evaluation to assess whether an idea is worth pursuing.

## Plan Types

| Plan | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Cost Plan** | Estimate the cost of developing the idea |
| **Budget Plan** | Approved spending for idea development |
| **Benefit Plan** | Expected returns if the idea becomes a project |
| **Forecast** | Updated cost projection combining actuals with ETC |

---

## Working with Cost Plans

Cost plans capture the estimated effort and expense required to develop or deliver the idea.

1. Navigate to the idea's **Financials** module
2. Select the **Cost Plans** tab
3. Click **+ New** to create a plan
4. Add line items by **resource**, **role**, or **expense type**
5. Enter planned amounts per period

> [!tip]
> Use **Populate > From Allocations** to auto-fill costs based on staffing allocations and rate matrices.

---

## Working with Budget Plans

Budget plans represent the approved spending baseline for idea development:

- **Promote** a cost plan to budget: Click **Actions** > **Promote to Budget**
- Track actual spending against the approved budget
- Compare cost vs. budget to monitor variance

---

## Working with Benefit Plans

Benefit plans model the expected returns or value if the idea is approved and executed:

- Enter expected revenue, cost savings, or other benefit types
- Compare the benefit plan against the cost plan for **ROI analysis**
- Use benefit data as part of the idea approval decision

---

## Working with Forecasts

Forecasts provide an updated view of expected costs as the idea progresses:

- Forecasts combine **actuals** (past) with **ETC** (estimated-to-complete)
- Variance = Budget - Forecast
- Updated regularly to reflect current expectations
