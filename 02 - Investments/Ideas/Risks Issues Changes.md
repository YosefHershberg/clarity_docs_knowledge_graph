# Manage Risks, Issues, and Changes for Ideas

## Overview

Risk, issue, and change management for ideas follows the same patterns as [[Risks Issues Changes|Custom Investment RIC]]. Refer to that note for full procedures including creating records, copying RICs, creating RICs from existing RICs, document uploads, and To Dos.

## Key Differences for Ideas

Ideas are evaluated before they become projects, so RIC management has a specific purpose at this stage:

| Item | Role in Idea Lifecycle |
|------|----------------------|
| **Risks** | Identified during idea development to help evaluate feasibility and viability |
| **Issues** | Confirmed problems that may influence the approve/reject decision |
| **Change Requests** | Requests to modify idea scope before conversion to a project |

> [!tip]
> Use Risks and Issues on ideas to build the risk register that will carry forward when the idea is converted to a project. This gives the project team an early view of known risks.

## Access Rights

Separate access rights are designated for copying and managing RICs related to Ideas (distinct from Projects, Custom Investments, and Team Investments).
