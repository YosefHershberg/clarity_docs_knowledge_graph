# Staff an Idea

## Overview

Staffing an idea works the same way as staffing other investments — see [[Staffing|Custom Investment Staffing]] for the full procedure, including adding resources, setting allocation percentages, booking types, and replacing or deleting staff.

## Key Points

- Add **named resources** or **placeholder roles** to model future staffing needs
- Set **allocation percentages** and **booking types**:
  - **Hard** — Committed allocation
  - **Soft** — Tentative/planned allocation
- Replace or delete staff members as the idea evolves
- Access rights control who can view or modify staffing on ideas

## Staffing an Idea Before Project Conversion

Staffing an idea enables early-stage capacity planning:

- Model the resource mix required if the idea is approved
- Use **Soft bookings** to reserve capacity without committing resources
- Staffing data can carry over when the idea is [[Getting Started#Converting Ideas to Projects|converted to a project]]

> [!tip]
> Use placeholder **Roles** rather than named resources during early idea evaluation, then replace with named resources once the idea is approved.
