# Getting Started with Team Investments

## Overview

Team Investments represent persistent, long-term funded teams organized around products and value streams rather than temporary projects. Instead of funding projects, organizations fund teams that deliver continuous value.

## Key Concepts

- **Persistent Funding** — Teams receive ongoing funding rather than project-based budgets
- **Value Stream Alignment** — Teams are organized around products, services, or business capabilities
- **Continuous Delivery** — Focus on ongoing value delivery rather than project completion

## Creating Team Investments

### From Scratch
1. Navigate to the **Team Investments** workspace
2. Click **+** to create a new team investment
3. Fill in team name, description, and attributes

### From Template
- Use templates for consistent team setup

### CSV Import (Beta)
- Bulk import team investments from CSV
