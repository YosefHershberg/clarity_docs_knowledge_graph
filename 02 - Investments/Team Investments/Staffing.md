# Staff a Team Investment

Team staffing follows the same patterns as other investment types. See [[Staffing|Custom Investment Staffing]] for full details.

## Team-Specific Considerations
- Teams typically have long-term, persistent staffing
- Resources may be fully allocated (100%) to a single team
- Mixed bookings support partial team membership
- Allocation values track team capacity over time
