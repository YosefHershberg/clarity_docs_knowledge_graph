# Team Investment Financial Plans

## Overview

Financial planning for team investments follows the same framework as other investments — see [[Financial Plans|Custom Investment Financial Plans]] for full procedures including creating cost plans, promoting to budget, working with forecasts, and benefit plans.

## Plan Types

| Plan | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Cost Plans** | Plan team operating costs (labor and non-labor) |
| **Budget Plans** | Approved spending baseline for the team |
| **Benefit Plans** | Track value delivered by the team |
| **Forecasts** | Updated cost projections combining actuals with ETC |

## Team-Specific Considerations

Teams have different financial patterns than time-bounded projects:

| Consideration | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Recurring budgets** | Teams often have annual or multi-year operational budgets rather than project budgets |
| **Multi-year planning** | Cost plans may span multiple fiscal years |
| **Value justification** | Benefit plans help justify continued funding for persistent teams (e.g., tracking features shipped, incidents resolved, or revenue enabled) |
| **Forecast cadence** | Forecasts are updated regularly to reflect current team capacity and backlog |

## Creating a Cost Plan for a Team Investment

1. Navigate to the team investment's **Financials** module
2. Click the **Cost Plans** tab
3. Click **+ New** to create a new plan
4. Add cost plan lines by resource, role, or expense type
5. Enter planned amounts per period
6. Optionally use **Populate > From Allocations** to auto-fill from staffing data

> [!tip]
> Use **Populate from Allocations** to keep cost plans synchronized with team staffing decisions.
