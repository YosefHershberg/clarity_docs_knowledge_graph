# Team Investment Financial Plans

Financial planning for team investments follows the same framework as other investments. See [[Financial Plans|Custom Investment Financial Plans]] for detailed procedures.

## Plan Types
- **Cost Plans** — Plan team operating costs
- **Budget Plans** — Approved team budgets
- **Benefit Plans** — Track value delivered by the team
- **Forecasts** — Updated projections

## Team-Specific Considerations
- Teams often have recurring operational budgets
- Cost plans may be annual or multi-year
- Benefit tracking helps justify persistent team funding
