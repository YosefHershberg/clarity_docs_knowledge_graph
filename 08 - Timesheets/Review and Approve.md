# Review and Approve Timesheets

## Approval Workflow
1. Navigate to Timesheets then Review and Approve
2. View submitted timesheets from your team
3. Review hours and notes
4. Click Approve or Return

## Returning a Timesheet
- Click Return and add a note explaining corrections needed
- Resource can then edit and resubmit
